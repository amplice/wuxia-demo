How Combat Works

One hit kills. Any clean weapon hit ends the round immediately.
Parry is on the block button. Tap block just before impact to parry. If you are early or keep holding, it becomes a normal block.
Blocks are safer, parries are stronger. Blocking absorbs the attack with knockback. A successful parry leaves the attacker open longer.
Position matters. Sidesteps and angles can beat linear attacks. Weapon clashes happen when active weapon paths collide.
In VS COMPUTER, Player 2 controls are unused. Toggle debug with F3.
